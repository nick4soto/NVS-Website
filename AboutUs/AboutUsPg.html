About Us

Our Company

NVS Corporate Services, Inc., established in 2001, is a premier building service contractor with a legacy of excellence. Recognized with numerous awards including the 2024 SBA Small Businessperson of the Year, we set industry standards in facility management. Certified as a HUBZone, Small Disadvantaged Business, and a leader in janitorial services, our expertise is acknowledged across the federal and private sectors.

We are committed to sustainability and cultural integrity, blending traditional values with modern practices. Our work with entities like the Department of Homeland Security and numerous educational institutions underscores our capability to manage complex facilities with a focus on safety, cleanliness, and efficiency. Each project is an opportunity to showcase our dedication to enhancing the operational quality and aesthetics of the environments we service.

Community Impact and Client Commitment

At NVS Corporate Services, Inc., our commitment to the community transcends everyday facility services. Rooted in Native American traditions and backed by a legacy of excellence since 2001, we enrich every project with a deep respect for cultural and environmental values. Our approach involves more than maintaining spaces; we transform them into areas that reflect the cultural richness and promote ecological sustainability. Sustaining Environments and Cultures

At NVS Corporate Services, Inc., sustainability is the core of our operations. As a Native American-owned business, we are guided by age-old traditions and respect for our ancestors' lands, harmoniously blending these principles with contemporary eco-friendly practices. Our use of biodegradable cleaning products, energy-efficient technologies, and advanced waste management strategies illustrates our commitment to reducing environmental impact. Every service we deliver is infused with this philosophy, ensuring that we not only preserve but enhance the ecological and cultural landscapes of the communities we serve.
