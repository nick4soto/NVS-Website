About Us

Our Company

NVS Corporate Services, Inc. was founded on January 1, 2000, in Brownsville, Texas, under the leadership of President & CEO Nick Soto Jr. With an extensive background in property development and facilities maintenance, our leadership brings invaluable expertise to every project. NVS is committed to blending Native American values with modern sustainability practices, making us a leader in both janitorial and facility services.

Our company has been recognized numerous times for our excellence, including the prestigious Small Business Person of the Year Award in 2024 awarded to Mr. Soto, and multiple certifications from the Small Business Administration as a testament to our commitment and quality. We pride ourselves on our innovative approach to traditional services, emphasizing environmental care and cultural respect.

Certifications & Associations:

Commitment to Certification and Excellence

NVS Corporate Services, Inc. has earned a reputation for excellence through rigorous standards and continuous improvement. We are proud to be certified by the Small Business Administration as a HUBZone contractor, Small Disadvantaged Business, and Totally Small Business. Our dedication to maintaining high standards is also reflected in our achievements such as the ISSA’s CIMS Certification in 2018, and our CIMS-GB certification for Green Cleaning in 2020. These certifications underline our commitment to quality and environmental stewardship, ensuring that we meet the highest industry standards for service and sustainability.

Community Impact and Client Commitment

NVS is committed to making a positive impact on every community we serve. By integrating Native American values into our business model, we do more than maintain facilities; we foster environments that reflect the cultural richness and environmental respect inherent in our traditions. Our commitment extends beyond business practices to active community engagement, ensuring that we not only meet but exceed the expectations of our clients and their constituencies. Our consistent growth through client referrals highlights our commitment to building lasting relationships based on integrity, performance, and mutual respect.

Environmental Stewardship and Cultural Integrity

Our operations are deeply integrated with our respect for the environment and our cultural heritage. NVS is a Native American owned company that combines traditional wisdom with modern innovation. We use eco-friendly cleaning products, energy-efficient equipment, and implement conscientious waste management practices to minimize our ecological footprint. Our efforts extend beyond simple cleaning—each service we provide resonates with respect for the land and a commitment to preserving the natural and cultural legacy of our community.

Industries We Serve

We serve a wide range of industries including commercial office buildings, local and federal government agencies, health care facilities, educational institutions, warehouse and logistics companies, and more.

We are a SBA HUBZone and SDBC Certified Business and GSA Schedule Contract Number GS-21F-0066V.
