Services

Repairs, Renovations & Construction

NVS Corporate Services’ Repair & Renovation department provides a wide range of building construction, repairs and renovation services. NVS has provided numerous services as a Prime Contractor for the General Service Administration from Brownsville to Laredo, TX. Our company has successfully completed numerous contract awards.

Construction & Renovations

Whether you need new commercial, institutional or industrial building construction, or have a building that needs to be remodeled, NVS can help. From conceptualization through project completion, NVS utilizes innovative engineering, skilled labor and proven project management to build cost effectiveness into each project and produce outstanding results.

Painting

A fresh coat of paint can give a building a new lease on life. NVS provides professional exterior and interior commercial painting services. No job is too big or too small. Our team provides professional preparation, high quality painting services and thorough clean-up. We know that our success is based on maintaining customer satisfaction through top-quality service.

Flooring

NVS Corporate Services offers full-service flooring installation solutions for commercial and institutional facilities. NVS is a single source solution for all your flooring needs whether you require tile, carpet, wood, laminate, vinyl or concrete flooring. Let us get your project from planning to completion as quickly and cost-effectively as possible.

Finish Carpentry

NVS takes pride in every detail of a construction or remodeling project. Our Finish Carpentry services put the finishing touches on any of our construction or remodeling projects. Whether it’s putting the trim and molding around doors and windows, hanging doors, attaching hardware, or building in shelves, you can rest assured NVS will get the job done right.

Electrical

NVS Corporate Services can provide commercial and industrial electrical services for projects of every size and scope. Whether you need new electrical construction, upgrades to existing wiring or a reliable energy back-up system, there is no electrical project or service outside our experience or ability.
