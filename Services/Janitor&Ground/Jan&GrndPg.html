Janitorial & Groundskeeping

NVS Corporate Services Inc. has nearly 25 years of experience in providing exceptional janitorial and groundskeeping services.

NVS is a CIMS Certified Company and CIMS Green Building Certified Company with Honors. The ISSA Cleaning Industry Management Standard (CIMS) provides a powerful tool to identify customer-focused and well-managed cleaning providers.

Enhanced Cleaning

NVS offers cleaning and disinfecting services that are effective against Covid-19, bacteria and other viruses. We use disinfectants that are EPA-Certified and CDC-Approved for use against Covid-19. Our services range from fogging to mitigate viruses and bacteria, to wiping down high-touch surfaces such as doorknobs, phones and light switches.

Janitorial

A clean, well maintained facility is essential to operations. Our cleaning professionals pride themselves on providing effective and high quality cleaning services. From sparkling floors and bathrooms to dust free, sanitized surfaces, NVS Corporate Services can evaluate your facility and develop a comprehensive and cost effective plan that meets your needs.

Lawn Care

Keep your facility’s exterior looking beautiful and well manicured with an ongoing commercial lawn maintenance plan from NVS. We can provide a comprehensive range of services including mowing, edging, trimming, pruning, property clean-up and more! We can create a customized lawn care plan that meets your needs and budget.

Landscaping

Your facility’s landscaping says a lot about your business. Whether your building is new or in need of a fresh appearance, NVS can provide beautifully designed landscapes designed to suit your needs.The landscape designs we create can incorporate ornamental plants and trees, flowering gardens, stonework, pavers, lighting, water features, and much more.

NVS is a CIMS Certified Business, a Small Business Administration certified HUBZone and Totally Small Business. NVS is also a GSA Schedule Contractor. We are proud members of ISSA and Building Services Contractors Association International (BSCAI). NVS was named the 8a Contractor of the year in 2020.
