CIMS Certified

What is CIMS Certification?

The ISSA Cleaning Industry Management Standard (CIMS) provides a powerful tool to identify customer-focused and well-managed cleaning providers. Independent, accredited assessors verify CIMS certified firms meet the industry standard for:

  • Quality Systems
  • Delivery
  • Human Resources
  • Health, Safety, and Environmental Stewardship
  • Management Commitment

Make your job easier and take your organization to the next level with a CIMS certified cleaning provider.

How does the CIMS Certification Benefit Your Business?

  • Allows you to choose a company that is truly professional and capable of getting the job done.
  • Assures you that our management systems and processes have been assessed by an independent third-party and that we comply with the industry’s leading standard.
  • Helps us deliver a consistently high level of service.
  • Enables us to reduce costs by improving efficiency, which allows us to pass along service improvements and savings to you.
  • Ensures that we have a sustainable business model that will provide you with valuable, long-term service.
  • And, most importantly, makes your job easier by helping you identify a quality, customer focused, professional organization.
