Other Services

NVS Corporate Services Inc. offers a wide range of services to help your business run smoothly. In addition to our janitorial and facility maintenance services, NVS offers services such as pest control, parking lot sweeping, business support services and much more.

Pest Control

NVS provides exceptional pest control services that eliminate unwanted pests quickly and efficiently. We will protect your building from pests like termites, ants, roaches, crickets, rats, mice, bees, wasps and more! Don’t let pests get in the way of your day-to-day operations. We provide treatments that are designed to be tough on pests while still being safe for your employees.

Parking Lot Sweeping

The exterior of your building makes a first impression. When visitors pull into your parking lot make sure the experience is a positive one. NVS can provide regularly scheduled parking sweeping services to maintain the parking surfaces surrounding your buildings. Our comprehensive parking lot services include sweeping, blowing and power washing if needed.

Business Services

NVS offers comprehensive office administration and business support services to facilities of all sizes. There is no job too small. We can handle color copies, copies, shredding, packaging and mailing services, and much more. Contact us today to assist with all of your business support services.

No Job Is Too Small!

We can provide a custom solution for your facility on a daily, weekly or monthly basis. Contact us today to get started.
