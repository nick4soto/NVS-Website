Our Work

Our Commitment to Excellence

Since our inception in 2001, NVS Corporate Services, Inc. has established itself as a leader in the building services sector, recognized for our commitment to excellence and a legacy of quality. As an Award-Winning, SBA HUBZone and Small Disadvantaged Business certified contractor, we serve a diverse clientele that includes top federal entities, educational institutions, healthcare facilities, and commercial spaces.

Our comprehensive facility services are guided by our deep respect for tradition and a forward-thinking approach to environmental stewardship, ensuring we meet the complex needs of each facility with precision and integrity. From janitorial services that set industry standards to specialized maintenance, every task is an opportunity to demonstrate our dedication to cleanliness, safety, and efficiency.

Our trusted partnerships with prestigious agencies such as the Department of Homeland Security and the US Navy highlight our industry credibility. Located in Historic downtown Brownsville, Texas, our training center stands as a cornerstone of our commitment to community and sustainability. This center, housed in a preserved 154-year-old building, symbolizes our dedication to conservation and heritage.

At NVS, we don’t just maintain facilities; we enhance them, weaving our values of respect, integrity, and excellence into every project to ensure they not only meet but exceed expectations. Our work is an embodiment of our mission to harmonize traditional wisdom with modern innovation, creating spaces that are not only functional but also nurturing.
